### I really love drawing in my sketchbook. 

I’m not sure yet whether it actually improves my mental state, or if it’s just a result of me already feeling better. But either way, when people gift me paints or sketchbooks, and I end up drawing with friends, I feel more alive somehow. I also like that it leaves behind traces of how I spend my time. yeah :)
