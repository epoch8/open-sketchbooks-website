### I’m an animation director from Moscow, currently living between Yerevan and Tbilisi. 

I graduated from VGIK and the SHAR Studio School. I create all the drawings in my films myself.

I’m not entirely sure what to write in an “about me” section right now, as I’m in the process of searching for another medium and experimenting with different forms.

### For me, a sketchbook is a kind of spontaneous stream of consciousness 

Less structured than a diary and freer than something created with an audience in mind.
