### I’m Vadim MosTwo, a graffiti artist and muralist 

I have 14 years of experience and am a member of the international graffiti collective ZNC.

I work in the direction of post-graffiti, also known as graffuturism. It emerged when graffiti moved beyond lettering and began absorbing other artistic styles and techniques.
