### I’m drawn to quick sketches, unfinished pieces, and experimenting with forms and materials. 

For me, keeping a sketchbook is a way of honestly capturing a moment through visual means, working with direct perception without the need to finish or perfect the result.

My focus is on everyday observations and spontaneous drawing. Through this practice, I explore process over outcome, trace my own internal shifts, build a connection with the surrounding space, and process experience through it – leaving a record along the way.
