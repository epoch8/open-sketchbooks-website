### My name is Olga Sokolova, I’m an art historian, writer, and artist. 

I write and illustrate my own books, ranging from children’s detective stories to explorations of mythology.

In art, I’m drawn to the spontaneous expression of the unconscious, ancient archetypes, mystical symbolism, and unexpected contrasts. I don’t limit myself to any single style or technique – I mix materials and love working with found and reused objects, from collages to drawings made on old cardboard packaging.
