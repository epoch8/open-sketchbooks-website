### I’m Katya, a copywriter, micro-horror author, and collage artist. 

I work with collage as a way of exploring images and states of mind.

I’m drawn to the ability to assemble new meanings from fragments of existing images – as if a new story emerges from scattered pieces. Alongside my personal work, I also run collage workshops, where I use it as a tool for visual thinking and creative experimentation.
