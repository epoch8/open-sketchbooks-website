### Artist, illustrator, and art historian. 

Graduated from an art college with a degree in fine arts and from Ural State University, Faculty of Art History. I have over 10 years of experience working in the museum field.

As an artist, I regularly participate in group exhibitions and illustrate books for private clients.

Projects and exhibitions include:

- Participation in projects of the Yekaterinburg branch of the National Centre for Contemporary Arts: *Night at the Museum*, *Art Factory* (2015)
- Participation in projects of the Gamayun Museum (2015)
- Participation in the exhibition *Russian House*, Art-Ptitsa Gallery (2016)
- Collaboration with Pole Gallery and Tatyana’s Day Auction House, Yekaterinburg (2016-2025)
- Work as a stage designer with Living Theatre (2018-2020)
- Participation in an exhibition at A3 Gallery, Moscow (2019)
- Collaboration with the German Consulate General in Yekaterinburg as an artist (2018-2021)
- Participation in the International Illustration Biennale, Haifa, Israel (2024)
- Participation in group exhibitions in Batumi, Georgia (2024, 2025, 2026)
