## About the event

This is the first open sketchbook exhibition in Tbilisi.

Not paintings.  
Not “final works”.  
Actual sketchbooks — the place where things start, break, and take shape.

You won’t just look at art on a wall.  
You’ll flip through it.

Page by page, you’ll see how ideas evolve — messy, precise, unfinished, alive.

Some sketchbooks feel like diaries.  
Some — like experiments.  
Some — like complete worlds.
