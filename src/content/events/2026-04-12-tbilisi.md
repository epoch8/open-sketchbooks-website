## About the event

The first open sketchbook exhibition in Tbilisi presents sketchbooks as complete works in their own right.

Visitors can follow how ideas take shape through sketches, experiments, and visual thinking, revealed page by page.

Each sketchbook carries its own style, rhythm, and narrative, forming a cohesive artistic statement.

Come to explore, flip through real sketchbooks, meet the artists, and spend time in a space centered on process and creative exchange.

## Why sketchbooks?

Sketchbooks are usually private.
They stay on shelves, in drawers, or inside backpacks.

This exhibition flips that.

It treats sketchbooks as first-class art objects — intimate, unfinished, alive.
